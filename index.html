About the Event

A Week Built for Builders

CCS Week is the premier annual event of the College of Computer Studies — a celebration of technology, creativity, and community. Every edition brings together students, faculty, and industry professionals for six days of meaningful competition and collaboration.

Whether you're a developer, designer, data scientist, or just curious about what's possible with technology, CCS Week has an event made for you.

  • Open to all CCS students across all year levels
  • Prizes, certificates, and industry recognition
  • Free registration for all official participants

Schedule

Six Days of Innovation

Every day packed with something to compete in, learn from, or simply enjoy.

Day 1
Monday, Mar 23

Opening Ceremony & Kickoff

Welcome remarks, event orientation, and the official start of CCS Week 2026. Meet your teams, collect your kits, and gear up.

Main Auditorium 8:00 AM
Day 2
Tuesday, Mar 24

Hackathon Begins + AI Exhibit

72-hour hackathon launches at noon. Alongside it, the AI Exhibit opens for public viewing — featuring student-built ML and AI projects.

CCS Lab Complex 12:00 PM
Day 3
Wednesday, Mar 25

Design Sprint & Web Dev Cup

Back-to-back design and development competitions. Morning slots for Design Sprint, afternoon for Web Dev Cup. Speed, creativity, execution.

CCS Rooms 201–204 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM
Day 4
Thursday, Mar 26

Tech Talk Series

Three keynote sessions from industry leaders and distinguished alumni covering AI, cybersecurity, and the future of software development.

Main Auditorium 10:00 AM
Day 5
Friday, Mar 27

Hackathon Finale & Presentations

Teams present their 72-hour builds to a panel of judges. Final pitches, demos, and live Q&A before the winners are announced.

CCS Gymnasium 12:00 PM – 6:00 PM
Day 6
Saturday, Mar 28

Awards Night & Closing Ceremony

A grand closing celebration — recognizing winners across all categories, featuring performances, special guests, and the passing of the torch for CCS Week 2027.

University Events Hall 6:00 PM

About the Event

Where Innovation Meets Community

NEXUS 2026 brings together the brightest minds in technology, design, and entrepreneurship for an unforgettable three-day experience.

NEXUS is Southeast Asia's fastest-growing tech summit, uniting developers, designers, entrepreneurs, and thought leaders under one roof. With a focus on actionable insights and real-world applications, NEXUS goes beyond conferences — it's a launchpad.

From immersive hands-on workshops to open-space hackathons and evening networking mixers, every moment is crafted to accelerate your growth and spark lasting collaborations.

Whether you're a student discovering your path, a seasoned professional leveling up, or a founder building the next big thing — NEXUS 2026 is your stage.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. If you can't find what you're looking for, reach out to our team.

Is registration free?
+
Yes! General registration for NEXUS 2026 is completely free. You get access to all keynotes, workshops, the expo floor, and networking events. Add-ons like the VIP Dinner and Certificate Bundle are optional paid upgrades.
Can I change my workshop selection later?
+
Yes. You can update your track preferences up to 7 days before the event via your confirmation email link. After that, changes are subject to availability at the registration desk.
Is the event in-person only?
+
NEXUS 2026 is primarily an in-person event at the BGC Convention Center. Selected keynote sessions will be livestreamed. Workshop seats are limited to registered in-person attendees only.
How do I join the Hackathon?
+
Check the "Join the NEXUS Hackathon" add-on during registration. You can join solo and be matched with a team, or register with a pre-formed team of up to 5 members using a shared team code.
What should I bring?
+
Bring a valid government ID for check-in, your confirmation QR code, your laptop, and chargers. We recommend bringing business cards or a QR code for easy contact sharing. Meals and snacks are provided for registered attendees.
Is there a refund or transfer policy?
+
General registration is free, so no refunds are necessary. For paid add-ons, refunds are available up to 14 days before the event. You may also transfer your registration to another person anytime before April 10, 2026.
